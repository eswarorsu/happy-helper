## 🎯 What is INNOVESTOR for Founders?

INNOVESTOR is your platform to:
- **Pitch** your startup idea to verified investors
- **Connect** with interested investors directly
- **Secure** funding through the platform
- **Track** your fundraising progress

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- [ ] Valid email address
- [ ] Phone number (for verification)
- [ ] Pitch deck ready (Google Drive link)
- [ ] Investment amount defined
- [ ] Team information ready
